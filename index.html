About Cash

Celebrity Chef. Entrepreneur. Singer. It all started for Emmanuel Cash at a young age when he discovered his passion for cooking. Growing up, his grandmother's house was his favorite place in the world. Whether it was catfish or smothered porkchops, as a little boy, Cash was always standing beside his grandmother, watching her every move. By the age of 12, he began preparing full course meals like lasagna, baked salmon, and homemade cheesecakes. With his given nickname "Sing Boy", Cash knew at that moment that he wanted to be a singing chef.
